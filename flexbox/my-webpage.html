Graeme Naylor Museum

The museum was built in 2009 and refurbished in 2016. It is devoted to the famous white lions of the greater Timbavati and It’s packed with interesting and fascinating displays that unlock many secrets of the natural history of the area.

Echo Caves

The Echo caves have been a shelter for humans since the middle stone age. The end of the cave has not yet been found and it is said that the end of the Echo Caves is somewhere close to the Strijdom Tunnel and The spectacular limestone and dripstone structures inside these natural caverns make for interesting imaginary figures, which can be seen in chambers throughout The Cave as it winds through the mountain.
